Drift Magazine

Drift Magazine was created to connect students at the University of Texas with the greater outdoor community.

We aim to explore subcultures, challenge viewpoints and highlight people.

Bringing nature inside.

Drift Magazine is an outdoor lifestyle publication at the University of Texas at Austin with a fervor for the outdoors. We strive to connect students with the environment and build a community of like-minded individuals.
