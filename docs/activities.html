Drift Magazine

Drift Magazine was created to connect students at the University of Texas with the greater outdoor community.

We aim to explore subcultures, challenge viewpoints and highlight people.

Swimming

The Barton Creek Greenbelt is home to many nature trails and swimming holes. Pack a lunch, a change of clothes and hit the trails, but be sure to take a load off and cool off in the crisp, clean water in the oasis of Austin.

Paddle Boarding

Paddle boarding is an all-time favorite outdoor activity of locals in Austin. Head on down to one of the many piers to rent a paddle board, or if you're really into it you can buy one (there's a ton of great inflatable ones), and then get out on the water and have a blast!

Produce Picking

My personal favorite outdoor activity: produce picking. Whatever season it is, there's always something to pick. Whether it be tulips, pumpkins or strawberries, you'll definitely have a blast with kids, family, a significant other or even by yourself!
